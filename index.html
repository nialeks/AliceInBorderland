Plot

A young unemployed gambler Arisu once again quarreled with his father and older brother and left home. He meets witsh two bosom friends — one is skipping work, and the other was fired — in the Shibuya area, where the guys see a strange salute in the sky, and after the trinity is covered by a new reality. Friends find themselves in a deserted version of Tokyo and find out that now they have to participate in brutal survival games, after successfully completing which they will be issued visas for several days. The losers will die, those who quit the game will die, and those who refuse to renew their visas will also face instant death from a heavenly laser.
